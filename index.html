the Neuroscience Vienna Network

The Vienna area harbors an exciting variety of neuroscience research. The Vienna Neuroscience Network is a regular meeting with scientific talks taking place every 4-6 months, organized on a rotational basis. The purpose is to enhance interactions and lead to scientific synergies between the different neuroscience research groups.

These meetings are not just for PIs. Younger researchers (PhD or postdoctoral level) are encouranged to particapte and to present their research.
